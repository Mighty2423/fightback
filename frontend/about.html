About FightBack

FightBack is a crowdsourced tool to help job seekers avoid online employment scams. We aim to create transparency and accountability in remote job markets by giving people the power to report suspicious listings and expose fake companies.

This app was built using Amazon Web Services (S3, EC2, RDS) to ensure global reach, high reliability, and secure data handling.
